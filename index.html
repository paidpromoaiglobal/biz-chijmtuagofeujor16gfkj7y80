About Us

SGP Academy

SGP Academy is a trusted tuition centre located in Kovilambakkam, Chennai, dedicated to providing quality education and personalised coaching to students from all grades and boards including CBSE, ICSE, and State Board.

Our experienced and passionate teachers create a supportive learning environment where every student is encouraged to ask questions, build confidence, and achieve their academic best. We focus on concept clarity, regular practice, and consistent progress.

  • Small batch sizes for personalised attention
  • Regular tests, assignments, and progress reports
  • CBSE, ICSE, and Tamil Nadu State Board coaching
